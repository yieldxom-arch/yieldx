🎯 YieldX – Final Subscription Structure Update

The subscription system should be structured as follows:

1️⃣ Student Subscriptions

Students can choose between:

Normal Plan

Pro Plan


Each plan should clearly define its features and limitations.


---

2️⃣ Organization Subscription

Organizations should have only one subscription plan.

There should not be multiple tiers for organizations.

Flow should be:

1. Organization creates an account.


2. Organization subscribes to the single available plan.


3. Payment is completed.


4. Subscription becomes active.


5. Organization gains access to:

Managing teachers

Viewing analytics

Administrative controls




If the subscription expires:

Access to organization features should be limited until renewal.



---

3️⃣ Role-Based Logic

Students → Choose between Normal and Pro.

Teachers → Linked under Organizations.

Organizations → Have one subscription plan that unlocks management features.


The goal is to keep the system simple, structured, and easy to manage without unnecessary subscription complexity.
